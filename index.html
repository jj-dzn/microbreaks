Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

⏱

Configurable timer

Set breaks every 20, 30, or 45 minutes — or pick a custom interval. The timer runs quietly in the background even when the popup is closed.

🧘

Animated stretch guides

15 office-friendly stretches with step-by-step instructions and animated figures showing you exactly how each movement works.

🎯

Strict mode overlay

When you need a real break, strict mode shows a full-screen overlay on your active tab — impossible to ignore, easy to dismiss.

🔥

Streak tracker

Track your daily break streak, total breaks taken, and minutes moved. Small numbers, big motivation.

💤

Snooze when you need to

Not a great moment? Hit snooze for 5 more minutes. No guilt, no nagging — just a gentle nudge when you're ready.

🌍

8 languages

English, Spanish, French, German, Hindi, Malayalam, Tamil, and Telugu. Auto-detects your browser language, or pick one yourself.

🔒

100% private

No accounts, no servers, no tracking. All your data stays on your device. MicroBreaks never sends anything anywhere.

How it works

Up and running in 30 seconds

1

Install the extension

Add MicroBreaks to Chrome from the Web Store. The timer starts automatically.

2

Pick your interval

Choose how often you want to be reminded — 20, 30, 45 minutes, or custom.

3

Get reminded

When it's time, you'll get a gentle notification with a stretch prompt.

4

Move and stretch

Follow the animated guide, take a breath, and get back to it feeling better.

Your privacy matters

MicroBreaks collects zero personal data. Everything — your settings, language preference, streak, and timer state — is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. Nothing is ever sent to a server.
